Radya Pustaka Museum

The Radyapustaka Museum has 12 rooms which all contain museum collections that are tens to hundreds of years old. The collection of the Radyapustaka Museum will open your eyes to the cultural heritage of the Indonesian people.

Keris Museum

The keris museum has around 409 Indonesian keris collections of various sizes and types. Not only examples of kerises, there is also a visual video showing the development of kerises from time to time.

Balekambang Park

Balekambang Park is a park built by KGPAA Mangkunegaran VII for his two daughters. This park has an area of ​​about 9.8 hectares, dominated by shady trees. Some are more than hundreds of years old.
